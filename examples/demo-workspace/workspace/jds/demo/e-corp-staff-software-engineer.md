## About E Corp

E Corp (ECorp Financial Group) is one of the largest diversified financial platforms in North America, processing millions of transactions daily across retail banking, institutional trading, and insurance products. Our Platform Intelligence team owns the tooling, automation, and AI systems that keep thousands of engineers productive and our reliability SLOs green.

We're not building AI for its own sake — we're building AI systems that measurably reduce the time engineers spend on toil so they can focus on product that moves the business.

## The Role

We're looking for a Staff Software Engineer to lead the next generation of AI-augmented operational tooling. You'll own design-through-delivery on systems that help our SRE and platform teams triage incidents faster, surface relevant context automatically, and automate repeatable approval workflows — all while keeping humans in the loop on consequential actions.

This is an IC staff role with meaningful cross-functional scope. You'll work directly with SRE leads, ops stakeholders, and product partners to identify the highest-leverage places to apply AI, prototype quickly, and ship production-grade systems. The role is funded, backfilled from a staff departure, and will have dedicated headcount to pair with.

---

## What You'll Own

- **AI-powered incident triage:** Design and operate a retrieval-augmented system that indexes runbooks, past postmortems, and system topology docs; on any high-severity alert, surfaces structured context to the on-call within seconds instead of minutes.
- **Agentic approval workflows:** Build production LLM-powered pipelines with explicit human-in-the-loop gates for high-impact actions (infrastructure changes, permission grants, escalation routing). The emphasis is on safe, auditable automation — not blind execution.
- **Developer experience tooling:** Automated PR reviews, knowledge-base search, and onboarding assist — systems that cut the cost of being new or context-switched.
- **Eval + observability:** Own the evaluation harness for every AI system you ship. Instrument retrieval quality, latency, and error modes. Drive continuous improvement on real production data.
