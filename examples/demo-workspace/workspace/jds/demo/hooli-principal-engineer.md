# Principal Engineer, AI Platform — Hooli

**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid — 2 days/week onsite required)
**Team:** Core AI Platform
**Level:** Principal Engineer (IC6)
**Compensation:** $250,000–$290,000 base + RSU + sign-on

---

## About the Role

Hooli's AI Platform team builds the internal infrastructure that powers AI features across every consumer and enterprise product — from search ranking to assistant experiences to developer tooling. We're looking for a Principal Engineer to own the architecture and roadmap for our multi-tenant LLM orchestration layer.

This is a high-ownership, high-visibility role. You'll define the abstractions that the rest of engineering builds on, work directly with product and infrastructure leadership, and lead a team of 4–6 engineers.

---

## What You'll Do

- Architect and own the multi-tenant LLM orchestration platform (request routing, context management, rate limiting, observability)
- Define the API contract between the AI Platform and consuming product teams; drive adoption
- Lead a team of 4–6 engineers across design, implementation, and production operations
- Partner with ML Research to integrate new models and retrieval techniques into the production stack
- Set the reliability, latency, and cost SLAs for AI-powered features at scale (tens of millions of end users)
- Drive cross-team alignment on AI tooling strategy; represent the platform at architecture review

---

## What We're Looking For

**Required:**
- 8+ years of software engineering experience, with 2+ years at staff or principal level
- Deep experience building and operating distributed systems at large scale
- Hands-on experience with LLM integration in production (RAG pipelines, tool-use, agents, or orchestration)
- Demonstrated ability to lead engineers and influence technical direction without always having direct authority
- Strong communication skills; can write crisp design docs and hold your own in architecture debates

**Preferred:**
- Experience building internal developer platforms or shared infrastructure teams consume
- Familiarity with vector databases, embedding pipelines, or retrieval-augmented generation in prod
- Experience with multi-tenancy challenges: isolation, quota enforcement, cost attribution

## The Team

You'll report to Marcus Webb, VP Engineering, AI Platform. The team owns three main surfaces: the LLM gateway, the retrieval stack, and the eval/observability layer. We operate as a platform team: our customers are internal product teams, not end users.
