## The Role

We're looking for a **Senior Software Engineer** to join our AI Tooling team, which owns the internal platforms that bring LLM-powered workflows to Abstergo's operational teams — procurement, research operations, legal, and HR. You'll design, build, and iterate on agentic workflows and RAG-based retrieval systems that real employees depend on daily.

This role sits at the intersection of platform engineering and applied AI: you'll ship production systems, not prototypes.

---

## What You'll Do

- Design and build production RAG pipelines over large internal corpora (documents, policy databases, contract repositories)
- Develop agentic tool-use workflows that automate high-friction operational tasks with human-in-the-loop checkpoints
- Integrate LLM services (API-based) into existing enterprise systems (ERP, HRIS, procurement tooling)
- Partner with operational stakeholders to scope, prototype, and validate AI-powered workflows before broader rollout
- Define and track quality metrics (retrieval accuracy, task-completion rate, latency) for owned systems
- Mentor junior engineers; participate in design reviews and architecture discussions
