**Pied Piper · Remote (US) · Full-time**

## About Pied Piper

Pied Piper invented middle-out lossless compression and has quietly become the backbone of enterprise data pipelines at Fortune 500 companies. Our enterprise tier serves 600+ customers across financial services, healthcare, and logistics — and we're now bringing AI-powered intelligence to the compression layer itself. Think: adaptive quality tuning, real-time anomaly detection, and AI-assisted integration workflows that cut onboarding from months to weeks.

We're a Series D company (130 engineers), remote-first with hubs in San Francisco and Austin.

---

## The Role

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join the Enterprise AI team, a cross-functional pod that owns the AI feature layer on top of PP's compression infrastructure, plus the integration surface that connects PP to customers' internal systems (Salesforce, Workday, custom ERPs, data lakes).

You'll work directly with enterprise customers during integration and build the production systems that make those integrations reliable and observable at scale.

**This is not a backend-SWE-in-a-silo role.** You'll be in customer calls, owning the technical translation from "what the customer needs" to "what we ship." Think of it as forward-deployed engineering with a strong product engineering backbone.

---

## What You'll Own

- Design and ship AI-powered features on PP's enterprise API layer — adaptive quality presets, compression-event workflow triggers, retrieval-augmented config recommendations
- Build and maintain customer integration connectors (Salesforce, Workday, custom webhook/REST/gRPC surfaces)
- Own observability and SLA compliance for the enterprise tier: alerting, runbooks, on-call rotation
- Drive technical discovery with enterprise customers during onboarding; translate requirements into reliable integration specs
- Collaborate with core-platform team on API contract evolution and versioning strategy
