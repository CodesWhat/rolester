## About InGen

InGen builds computational biology infrastructure for drug discovery. Our AI Platform team
owns the ML-serving layer, internal data tooling, and the developer platform used by 200+
scientists and engineers.

## The Role

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to help build and scale the systems that power
InGen's AI-assisted research pipelines. You'll work across ML serving, internal APIs, and
developer tooling — making it faster and safer for scientists to run experiments and interpret
results.

## What You'll Do

- Design and operate production ML-serving infrastructure (model endpoints, batching, caching)
- Build internal tooling and APIs that surface AI-generated insights to non-eng stakeholders
- Collaborate with bioinformatics teams to translate research prototypes into production systems
- Own reliability and observability for the AI platform layer

## What We're Looking For

- 5+ years backend/SWE experience; strong Python or Go
- Experience taking ML prototypes to production (RAG, inference pipelines, or similar)
- Comfort working directly with non-engineering users and translating ambiguous requirements
- Familiarity with LLM APIs and agentic workflow patterns is a strong plus
