# Staff Engineer — AI Platform
**Massive Dynamic** | Remote (US) | Full-time

---

## About Massive Dynamic

Massive Dynamic builds enterprise-grade AI infrastructure — the scaffolding that lets Fortune 500 teams move from LLM prototype to reliable, auditable production. Our AI Platform team owns the retrieval layer, the prompt management SDK, and the integration surface that dozens of internal product teams and enterprise customers depend on.

We've grown from 400 to 1,100 employees in two years. The AI Platform team is 14 engineers; this Staff role is a new headcount driven by platform scale.

---

## The role

You'll be the technical anchor for the LLM integration layer of our AI Platform — the layer that sits between foundation models and the product surfaces our customers ship. That means:

- **Technical leadership:** Set architecture direction for retrieval infrastructure (hybrid search, reranking, embedding pipelines), context assembly, and the internal prompt SDK. Mentor 3–5 senior engineers.
- **Cross-functional scope:** Partner with data science (model evaluation, evals harness), enterprise customer success (integration patterns, SLA design), and product (roadmap input, API surface contracts).
- **Hands-on delivery:** Write production code weekly. We don't have Staff roles that are pure architecture; you'll own components end-to-end.
- **Platform reliability:** Define and hold SLAs for retrieval latency (P95 <400ms) and context-assembly throughput. Drive incident review and postmortems.

---

## What we're looking for

**Required**
- 8+ years of software engineering; 2+ years influencing architecture at scale (Staff or Principal track)
- Production experience building retrieval or knowledge-graph systems at non-trivial corpus size (≥1M documents)
- Hands-on work with LLM APIs in production (prompt engineering at scale, context management, streaming, tool use)
- Strong distributed systems fundamentals: consistency trade-offs, async processing, queue-based architectures
- Track record leading cross-functional initiatives without direct authority

**Nice to have**
- Experience in a forward-deployed or customer-facing eng role (discovery, integration with customer stacks)
- Familiarity with enterprise data environments (Salesforce, Workday, Snowflake integrations)
- Vector database experience (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector, Qdrant)
- Evaluation harness design for LLM outputs (LLM-as-judge, human-spot-check pipelines)
