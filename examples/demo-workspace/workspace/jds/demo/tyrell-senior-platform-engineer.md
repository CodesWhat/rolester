## About Tyrell Corporation

Tyrell Corporation builds next-generation biotech infrastructure software — think ERP meets laboratory information management meets AI-assisted research tooling. Our platform processes millions of research events daily across 40+ enterprise customers in pharma, agri-biotech, and clinical diagnostics.

We're scaling rapidly after our Series C and need platform engineers who can own large, customer-facing systems end-to-end — not just ship features but embed with customers to make the product stick.

---

## The Role

As a **Senior Platform Engineer** you'll sit at the intersection of product, customers, and infrastructure. You'll own the integration layer that connects Tyrell's platform to external enterprise systems (ERP, LIMS, Salesforce, HL7 feeds), drive new customer deployments from kickoff through go-live, and work directly with customer engineering teams to debug integration issues and accelerate adoption.

This is **not** a pure backend SWE role — you'll be in customer calls, leading technical discovery, and writing code that lives in production at Fortune 500 accounts.

---

## What You'll Do

- Lead technical onboarding for Tier-1 enterprise customers: discovery → config → integration → hypercare
- Build and maintain the Tyrell Integration Layer (REST + webhook + ETL connectors to SAP, Salesforce, Veeva, custom LIMS)
- Design and operate the AI-assisted workflow engine (LLM-augmented data normalization, anomaly flagging, report generation)
- Instrument deployments with observability tooling; own SLOs for customer-facing latency and uptime
- Collaborate with Customer Success and Sales Engineering on pre-sales technical evaluations
- Contribute to the platform roadmap — you're close to customers and your input shapes the product

---

## Requirements

- 5+ years production software engineering; 2+ years in a customer-facing or forward-deployed capacity
- Strong Python and/or TypeScript; REST API design; familiarity with ETL patterns
- Hands-on experience with at least two of: Salesforce, SAP, Workday, HL7, Veeva
- Comfort operating in ambiguous early-deployment environments (no perfect runbook)
- Excellent written and verbal communication — you'll represent Tyrell in customer calls

- Prior work in biotech, pharma, or LIMS-adjacent domains
