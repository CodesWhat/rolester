# Principal Engineer — Omni Consumer Products

**Location:** New York, NY (Onsite)
**Team:** Platform Engineering
**Level:** Principal (IC5)
**Compensation:** $250,000–$290,000 base + equity + bonus

---

## About OCP

Omni Consumer Products builds enterprise automation platforms that power city-scale infrastructure operations — fleet management, resource allocation, and compliance reporting for municipal and public-sector clients across 40+ metro areas. Our Platform Engineering team owns the core services, data pipelines, and integrations that every OCP product runs on.

## The Role

We're looking for a Principal Engineer to lead the modernization of our automation platform — a system that coordinates real-time decisions across city infrastructure with strict reliability and governance constraints. You'll set the technical direction, partner with product and ops leadership, and be the engineer stakeholders trust when something breaks at 2am (or when a city council wants an audit trail).

This is a high-ownership IC role. You'll work closely with VP of Platform Engineering Marcus Whitfield and a cross-functional team that includes public-sector compliance leads, product managers, and field ops.

## What You'll Do

- Architect and deliver the next-generation automation platform replacing our legacy batch-processing core with event-driven, real-time services
- Define reliability standards (SLOs, incident response, runbooks) for systems that operate under public-sector SLAs
- Lead technical design reviews and establish engineering standards across 4 platform teams (40+ engineers)
- Partner with compliance and legal to ensure auditability, data governance, and regulatory alignment
- Drive integration strategy for third-party city systems (311, fleet management APIs, permitting systems)
- Mentor senior engineers; player-coach model on high-stakes delivery

## What We're Looking For

- 8+ years of backend engineering; 3+ years at staff or principal level
- Experience building and operating high-reliability distributed systems at scale
- Comfortable navigating governance/compliance constraints — audit trails, access controls, chain-of-custody
- Strong track record of technical leadership across multiple teams
- Experience with enterprise integrations and customer-facing delivery a plus
- Public-sector or regulated-industry experience a plus (not required)
